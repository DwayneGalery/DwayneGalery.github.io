GET ON TOP

Overview

Get on top is a game where you 1v1 someone and who ever gets on top wins.

How to play

FIRST TO GET ON TOP WINS

1. Player 1 controls are Left=A Right=D Jump=W

2. Player 2 controls are Left= left arrow Right= Right arrow Jump= Up arrow

3. To play on moblie devices you can use the arrow buttons on the screen
