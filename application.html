GET ON TOP

Overview

Get on top is a game where you 1v1 someone and who ever gets on top wins.

How to play

FIRST TO GET ON TOP WINS

  • Player 1 controls are Left=A Right=D Jump=W
  • Player 2 controls are Left= left arrow Right= Right arrow Jump= Up arrow
  • To play on moblie devices you can use the arrow buttons on the screen
